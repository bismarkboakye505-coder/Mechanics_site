Inclined Planes

On a smooth incline at angle θ, the weight mg resolves into components: mg sinθ down the slope and mg cosθ perpendicular to the slope.

Key Relations

  • Down-slope component: F‖ = mg sinθ
  • Normal reaction: R = mg cosθ
  • With friction (coefficient μ): F_f = μR = μmg cosθ

Worked Example

A 4 kg block on a 30° smooth incline accelerates at

a = g sin 30° = 9.8 × 0.5 ≈ 4.9 m/s²
