Welcome

This website is a web-based learning resource for Senior High School students in Ghana. It focuses on four core subtopics in Mechanics: Laws of Motion, Inclined Planes, Pulley Systems, and Forces. It was designed using Adobe Dreamweaver and plain Notepad, meeting the ICTD 361 project brief.

Purpose

  • Provide concise notes, visuals and solved examples.
  • Offer short quizzes for instant feedback.
  • Give assignments and space for comments and questions.

Target Users

SHS students and teachers who want curated, exam-focused content on Mechanics topics.
