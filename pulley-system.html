Pulley System (Atwood Machine)

Consider two masses m₁ and m₂ connected by a light inextensible string over a smooth pulley. The system accelerates with

a = (m₂ − m₁)g / (m₁ + m₂) and tension T = 2m₁m₂g / (m₁ + m₂).

Assumptions

  • String is light and inextensible.
  • Pulley and axle are smooth (no friction).
  • Masses move in straight lines with common magnitude of acceleration.

Example

If m₁ = 2 kg and m₂ = 6 kg, then

a = (6−2)×9.8 / (6+2) = 4×9.8/8 = 4.9 m/s²
