Forces & Free‑Body Diagrams

Common forces in Mechanics include weight (mg), normal reaction, tension, friction, thrust and drag. A free‑body diagram (FBD) shows all external forces acting on a body.

Drawing an FBD

  1. Isolate the body.
  2. Show weight acting at center of mass.
  3. Add normal reaction at contact surfaces.
  4. Add friction opposing relative motion.
  5. Add applied forces and tension(s).

Equilibrium

For equilibrium: ΣF_x = 0 and ΣF_y = 0, and for rigid bodies, Στ = 0.
